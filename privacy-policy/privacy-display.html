Privacy Policy: Privacy Display

Last updated: February 27, 2026

IonXtech ("we", "our", or "us") built the Privacy Display app as a utility application. This Service is provided by IonXtech and is intended for use as is.

This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Privacy Display is designed to work without collecting or storing personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos or media files
  • Browsing history
  • Keystrokes
  • Screen contents
  • App usage content data

The app operates locally on your device.

2. Permissions We Request and Why

To function properly, Privacy Display requires certain system permissions. These permissions are used strictly for core functionality.

Display Over Other Apps (Overlay Permission)

Used to display the privacy screen above other apps. We do not read or capture the content of other apps.

Foreground Service

Required to keep the privacy overlay active while the app runs in the background.

Notification Permission (Android 13+)

Used to show a persistent notification that allows you to quickly toggle the privacy overlay.

Receive Boot Completed

If you enable the "Start on Boot" option, this permission allows the app to restart the privacy service after your device reboots.

Usage Access (Optional, If Enabled)

If you enable automation features that depend on app detection, the app may request Usage Access permission to detect which app is currently in the foreground. We do not collect or store this data. It is processed locally on your device to trigger overlay automation.

Ignore Battery Optimizations (Optional)

If you choose to disable battery optimization for uninterrupted service, this permission ensures the overlay works reliably in the background.

3. Data Storage

All settings (such as overlay opacity, automation preferences, and scheduling) are stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms.

No data is transmitted to IonXtech or third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share personal data with third parties
  • Use third-party analytics
  • Use advertising SDKs
  • Transmit data to external servers

Privacy Display operates entirely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

Privacy Display does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data, it is safe for general audiences.

6. Security

Because the app does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk related to data breaches. All functionality runs locally on your device.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
