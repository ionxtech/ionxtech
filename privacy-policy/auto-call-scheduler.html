Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Auto Call Scheduler mobile application developed by IonXtech handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

The Auto Call Scheduler app allows users to schedule phone calls at a selected date and time. The application operates primarily using device features and stores all necessary information locally on the user’s device.

1. Information We Collect

The Auto Call Scheduler application does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All scheduled call data remains stored locally on the user’s device.

The app may access certain device features only to provide its core functionality.

2. Permissions Used

The application may request the following permissions:

  • CALL_PHONE
    This permission allows the application to initiate a phone call automatically at the scheduled time set by the user.
  • READ_CONTACTS (Optional)
    If the user chooses to select a phone number from their contacts, the app may request access to contacts. This permission is used only to allow the user to select a contact and is not used to collect or store contact information externally.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
    This permission allows the app to trigger scheduled calls at the exact time selected by the user.

All permissions are used strictly for the core functionality of the application.

3. Data Storage

All scheduled call data such as phone numbers, date, and time are stored locally on the user's device.

IonXtech does not store user data on external servers or cloud databases.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

All information remains fully controlled by the user on their device.

5. Third-Party Services

The Auto Call Scheduler app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

6. Security

We value user privacy and take reasonable measures to protect user data. Since all information is stored locally on the device, security also depends on the security of the user's device.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
