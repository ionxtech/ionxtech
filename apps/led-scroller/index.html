LED Scroller

Scrolling LED Display App

LED Scroller turns your Android phone into a bright, eye-catching scrolling LED sign. Type any message and watch it scroll across your screen in large, readable text — perfect for concerts, events, protests, airports, or anywhere you need to be seen without shouting.

  • Customisable text, font size, and scroll speed
  • Multiple colour themes and background options
  • Works offline — no internet required
  • No personal data collected
